About Us.

Happy lips is a makeup firm based in Hyderabad. The main motto of happylips is to provide the best quality makeup products for a very reasonable price over the products available in the market and to promote home business. Our disclosed partners will provide us with the best raw materials. And our happylips team will handle materials with expertise, hygiene; and mix the colors to precision. Our team is always in the race to introduce new quality colors and products.
